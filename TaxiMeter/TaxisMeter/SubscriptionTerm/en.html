Member Subscription Instructions


Confirm subscription: After the user confirms the purchase and payment, it will be credited to the iTunes account.

Manage subscription: Users can manage or cancel subscriptions in the iTunes & App Store account management in the phone system settings.

Automatic renewal: Apple iTunes account will be deducted for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period. If you need to cancel the renewal, please cancel manually 24 hours before the expiration of the current subscription period. The current subscription cannot be canceled during the valid subscription period.

Free trial: (If any) the free trial will automatically renew the premium version after it expires. You can go to the account settings to cancel the renewal before the trial ends. If you pay for the premium version before the trial period ends, the current trial will end automatically.
